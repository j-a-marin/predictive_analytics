2014 Philharmonia.org Kaggle Competition

Purpose of competition was to help boost sales and find pockets of best clientele amongst past attendees.

Gradient Boosting Ensemble algorithm was implemented. Gradient Boosting is a machine learning technique based upon weak predictive decision trees on an optimization overlay of a differentiable loss function. The technique was originally developed by Leo Breiman and expanded by Jerome Friedman.
